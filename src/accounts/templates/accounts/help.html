Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

You can either scroll down to see the Start button, or click the Start tab to be redirected to analyzer page.

What is included in the evaluation?

Our evaluation analyzes several aspects of candidates' resume, including name, mail, phone number, skills, experience and more. You'll receive a score and contact information of top 3 Resumes.

Are there any in-app purchases?

Currently, our web application is free of charge, however, future upgrades with paid content are possible.

How can I view my history?

You can view your results history by clicking on History tab button in the menu bar. Please note that you need to save the results, otherwise, data will be lost.

Are other languages supported in this app?

This web application will soon be upgraded to a newer version, thus, more language options will be available!

What if there is a problem or suspicious activity in the account?

In this case, we offer a help by providing users with our contact information, i.e. mail, phone number.
