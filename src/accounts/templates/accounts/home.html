    Instructions:
    1. Scroll down and find Start button/click on Start tab in the menu bar in order to get to the analyzer page
    2. On the left side, click the "Choose file" button, and select one or more files to upload (Note: only doc/docx files are accepted)
    3. On the right side, in the given box, write down the requirements of the job which the candidates must satisfy (Please split the requirements with ',', or the scores will be displayed as 0.00)
    4. To start the analysis press "Compare" button
    5. In the results page you will have two options: Save Results/Start New Submission.
    6. In order to view saved results, click on the "History" tab in the menu bar.
    7. You can clear history data by clicking "Clear History" button.
    8. If you need any help or our contacts, write "Help" or "Contact" in the search bar.
